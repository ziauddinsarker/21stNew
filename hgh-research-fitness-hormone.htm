NEW LANDMARK RESEARCH

In a new study, researchers compared HGH levels in several ways ... resting (for a baseline measurement), after a 6 second cycle sprint, and after 30 second cycle sprint. They also measured it for hours afterwards to see how long human growth hormone stayed in participant's bodies after exercise, (The time course of the human growth hormone response to a 6s and a 30s cycle ergometer sprint, 2002, Stokes).

THE RESULTS ARE INCREDIBLE!
