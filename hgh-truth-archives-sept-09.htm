This allows the supplement to be absorbed effectively while maintaining the structural and conformational integrity of the human growth hormone peptide. After looking at your site you state the following: “The homeopathic dilution process plus the inclusion of alcohol in the oral spray breaks down the molecular structure of Human Growth Hormone so that HGH 30X CAN enter the body through the oral mucosa. If you do this then are you not effectively destroying the peptide? If it is destroyed how can it be effective. Why do you not deliver it via liposome delivery method?

I am very serious about purchasing this product and would like detailed answers to my questions. I have a graduate degree in molecular biology and microbiology so there is no need to hold back on the details. The more information I get the better.

Thank you in advance for answering my questions.
